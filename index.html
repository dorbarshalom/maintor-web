Platform Capabilities

Everything industrial maintenance demands.

Built on Cloudflare Workers — serverless, globally distributed infrastructure delivering sub-second response times even from the factory floor.

📱

Mobile-First Technician App

Large tap targets, offline-resilient logging, and a UI so simple it requires zero onboarding. Built exclusively for the factory floor, not a browser port.

💻

Admin Command Center

Comprehensive desktop dashboard with scheduling calendars, custom data columns, filterable asset tables, and full role management (Owner, Admin, Site Manager).

💬

WhatsApp Notification Engine

Configurable template-based alerts sent directly to WhatsApp. No app install required from recipients. Critical breakdowns demand immediate attention, not inbox scanning.

🤝

Account-to-Account Invitations

Invite external contractors or consultants directly from their own Maintor account to collaborate on your sites — with role-scoped access and zero additional seat costs.

⏱

Precision Labor & Downtime Tracking

Captures start/end times and duration per assignee on every ticket. Automatically calculates exact machine downtime in minutes for accurate OEE reporting.

📊

Compliance Audit Trails

Every maintenance action is logged with timestamps, photo evidence, and technician sign-offs — export-ready for ISO, food safety, or pharmaceutical compliance audits.
